A "simple" Hamburger Recipe that mostly anyone can follow!

Just as the title says it, this recipe will show you how to cook a simple hamburger if you are in the mood for one and have the resources to make one.

you will need a grill, whatever type is up to you as long as you can cook on it.

INGREDIENTS:

  • 1. buns
  • 2. ground beef
  • 3. cheese (sliced)
  • 4. pickles
  • 5. sliced tomato
  • 6. lettuce
  • 7. whatever else you want on it

now that you have these igredients, heat up your grill and follow these simple steps:

  • 1. Take a chunk of ground beef and roll it into a ball
  • 2. mash the ball until flat, this is the pattie, the most important part.
  • 3. make as many patties as you want and put them on the grill.
  • 4. cook them until they are at your preferred state (medium well, rare etc.)
  • 5. put mayonnaise on the buns and put them in the oven to toast.
    • ! Leave the oven open so you don't burn the buns, watch them and take them out when they are brown!
  • 6. take out the buns then put the patties on the buns
  • 7. put cheese, lettuce, tomatoes, onions, whatever you prefer on the burger
Congratulations! you made one or more hamburgers.
